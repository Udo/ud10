#Physiological Skills+

Unless otherwise stated in templates, taking these skills should be subject to GM review.

##Resistance: (specify)
Characters with the Resistance skill have an innate resistance to a specific agent which otherwise adversely affects most other people. Examples for Resistance skills would be: toxins, radiation, cold, heat, diseases, magic, fear, insanity, revulsion.
